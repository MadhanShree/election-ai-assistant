📝 First-Time Voter Guide

1

Check Eligibility

You must be an Indian citizen and 18+ years old on the qualifying date (usually Jan 1st).

2

Register Online/Offline

Fill out Form 6 on the NVSP portal (voters.eci.gov.in) or submit it to your local ERO.

3

Get Voter ID (EPIC)

Once verified, your name is added to the electoral roll and you receive your Voter ID.

4

Find Polling Booth

Check the ECI website or app to find your designated polling station and booth slip.

5

Cast Your Vote

Take your ID to the booth, verify identity, press the EVM button, and check the VVPAT slip.

🛑 Fake Election News Awareness

Misinformation can derail democracy. Learn how to spot fake news.

  • Verify the Source: Is it a reputed news outlet or an unverified WhatsApp forward?
  • Check for Emotion: Fake news often uses highly emotive language to spark outrage.
  • Reverse Image Search: Images and videos are often taken out of context. Use tools like Google Lens to trace origins.
  • Check Dates: Old news is frequently recycled as "breaking" news during election time.
  • Consult Fact-Checkers: Follow ECI verified handles and recognized fact-checking websites.

How the Prediction Works:

The model uses a weighted formula to calculate a total score for each candidate, which is then normalized into a predicted vote share percentage. The weights are:

  • Current Vote Estimate: 50% (Base popularity metric)
  • Previous Share: 20% (Historical performance baseline)
  • Campaign Score: 10% (Momentum and outreach quality)
  • Youth/Women/Urban/Rural Support: 5% each (Demographic impact metrics)
